a lovely idea from Destiny Hankerson for anyone to try

After putting a call out for collaborations, one of the first replies was from Destiny Hankerson, a fellow creative person with a mixed array of creative output which she shares on her site. I was particularly taken by her lovely idea: The Human Acceptance Project (embedded below) where she expresses positive vibes in poetry and drawings.

I thought it would be a great opportunity to invite others to do the same, and submit their own "Human Acceptance Project". If you have something to share, it can be simple, complex, personal or universal, send me a link
