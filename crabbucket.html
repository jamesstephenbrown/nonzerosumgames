CRABS IN A BUCKET!

This is a classic illustration of a negative-sum system whereby crabs pull each other down in an attempt to escape a bucket resulting in no crabs escaping.

There are two ways of looking at this illustration

1. Similar to Tall Poppy Syndrome whereby people are pulling others down to their level, purely out of spite or to make themselves feel better relative to others.

2. A Moloch-y system whereby the narrow individual imperative to escape results in an incentive to use others to climb out, which results in no one escaping.

The counterpoint to this illustration is a situation where crabs (which are quite capable of holding on to each other) can form a ladder, and then a sort of rolling chain to enable the escape of everyone.
