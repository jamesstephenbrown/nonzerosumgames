IDENTITY POLITICS

While there is a confusing fracture of the left and right into globalist vs localist, other microcosms of protectionism have arisen across the spectrum. Identity politics is the adoption of political views based on group allegiance rather than reason. There is a popular centrist intellectual position, highlighted often by some members of the Intellectual Dark Web that identity politics on the right manifests in very visible white nationalism while on the left it appears as more pernicious social justice warriors on university campuses.
