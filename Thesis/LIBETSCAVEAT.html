LIBET'S CAVEAT

Of course conclusions are usually more complicated than they first appear. It turns out that after Libet showed that our behaviour precedes conscious awareness, later experiments found that halting of behaviour could happen after when he had deemed the behaviour had been decided on. But that halting still came a fraction of a second before the participants recorded that they were consciously aware of halting the decision. So the idea is still worth entertaining.
