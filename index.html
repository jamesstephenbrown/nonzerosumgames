James Brown

Documentary Director / Editor

Kia Ora — I began Branch Out Media in 2007 when I left an easy-going job making education videos to branch out on my own as a freelance director and editor. I am based in Auckland, New Zealand, but work with clients around the globe telling stories, big and small.

Over the past 20 years I have edited for many of New Zealand's top documentary directors and producers — Leanne Pooley, Annie Goldson, Cass Avery, Oskar Kightly, Roger Donaldson, Pietra Brettkelly, Justin Pemberton, Matthew Metcalfe and many others.

I've received three national awards for documentary editing: 2013 Moa with director Annie Goldson, 2020 NZTVA with co-editor Prisca Bouchet, and 2025 NZSA with my long-time editing partner Tim Woodhouse.

As a director I have shot all over the world, primarily on my long-form series "An American Rugby Odyssey", filmed in New Zealand, USA, China, UK, Europe, Japan, Brazil, Fiji and Tanzania.

If you're looking for editing services please check out my rate and availability.
