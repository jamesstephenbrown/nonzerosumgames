FRIENDGIBLES

This is all pretty new to me but here goes. Basically I drew 13 elements and wrote a javascript file to combine those drawings to make different faces in 5 different colours and came up with 10,000 unique pictures and IMHO they looked pretty rad.

A first set of 30 are up on Opensea.io/FRIENDGIBLES. They're "Friendgibles" because they're a set of friends who are both Non-fungible in the sense that they are Non-Fungible Tokens and fungible in the sense that their materials are interchangeable.

They are a great way to get into the NFT market as they are starting out at a very low price ;) and collections are all the rage at the moment, apparently - I don't really know anything about NFTs to be honest, except what I'm learning along the way. While these are on Opensea.io, I actually minted them on Nifty Mints, who make it easy to bulk upload NFTs to the blockchain, and offer you your first 30 free.
