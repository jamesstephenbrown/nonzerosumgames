THE HORRID BATH

It was made from the crystals that were finest, but it wasn't that that was troubling me, it was what was inside it.

I knew, by the look of it, it was made by the eyeballs of cochroaches, it was like olive mash.

Someone who liked olives would say I was lucky, but it was the colour and... imagine bathing in it!

I step in...

Suddenly the water changed to rainbow water.

The next day I see the bath, I imagine the olive colour, then the rainbow. I jump in...

Oops there's no water, I hit the hard crystal floor, I've sprained my ankle.
