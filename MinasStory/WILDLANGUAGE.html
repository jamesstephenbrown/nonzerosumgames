WILD LANGUAGE

I've been running for hours now, when finally I see a pig.

"Hello..." she says "you might be wondering why I can speak. Well, I changed brains with a hunter - now he thinks he's a pig! Ha"

The pig giggles, she seems nice. Then she says "Here, I'll take you to my friend Life she's a dragon".

I...

... refuse and run back up to the house
... say yes and go with the piglet to meet Life
... say I'll be able to cope, then run back home
