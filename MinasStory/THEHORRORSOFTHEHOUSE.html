THE HORRORS OF THE HOUSE

The door makes a creaking noise as I push it open. I step inside. The door slams behind me, inches from my head. I hear a voice...

"Stupid little girl, how silly you were to come to my home. Get out and I will spare you" the voice says.

I'm scared, the voice sounded horrible, but still, I raise my fists, ready to fight. I think about asking it to come and show itself. I don't know, so I...

... continue walking onwards
... run to my friends houses
... ask it to come out anyway!
