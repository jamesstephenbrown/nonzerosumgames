WHAT IT'S LIKE BEING A GHOST

I go straight through her - what? She's a ghost! But Death's words scare me, so I run. I hear Death say "Thank you". I slam the door shut behind me, run out of the gates and head home.

As I run home I wonder what it would be like to be a ghost, I open the front door of my house, step inside and head straight for bed!

THE END.
