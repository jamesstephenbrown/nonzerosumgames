A WEE THOUGHT ABOUT THE BENEFITS OF SUBSCRIBING TO STUFF

We live in an online world facilitated by algorithms, choosing for you what appeals most to people like you. But that means you're not just being served what's generally popular—you're being shaped by it. Subscribing is not just a way of supporting creators you value, but it's a way of restoring your own agency in what is served to you, allowing your interests to determine what becomes popular rather than what's popular determining your interests.
