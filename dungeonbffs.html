Dungeon BFFs

Dungeon BFFs is a board game my daughter and I have been developing, where you rescue your friends from a dungeon that you and the dungeon master collaborate in making - the objective is to escape the dungeon with your friends, without getting enclosed by walls.



We began prototyping with squares made from corrigated cardboard and vivid marker, and Mina's Hatchimals, and then graduated to a printed version from Board Games Maker




At present this is the only board game we're making, if you'd like a copy we'll be happy to provide you a current build, and would love to hear your feedback as we develop it. Comment below :)
