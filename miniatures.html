PAINTING MINIATURES

I love painting miniatures and use them when playing Dungeons and Dragons with my daughter. Here are some of my favorites. Most of them are from an old HEROQUEST (the greatest game ever made, and anyone who says otherwise is wrong) set

And so, with the means to buy 'proper' Citadel paints with 'proper' names like 'Lead Belcher' or 'Screaming Skull' I was on my way!
