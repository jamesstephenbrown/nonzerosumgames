PAINTING MINIATURES

I love painting miniatures and use them when playing Dungeons and Dragons with my daughter. Here are some of my favorites. Most of them are from an old HEROQUEST (the greatest game ever made, and anyone who says otherwise is wrong) set









I used to paint in my teens when I had no money and only access to a hobby shop that had gloss enamels for painting WWII aeroplanes and submarines, and so when I opened up my Heroquest game to introduce my daughter to my favorite childhood game, and show off my painting talent, they looked pretty much like those you see in the video above. And so, with the means to buy 'proper' Citadel paints with 'proper' names like 'Lead Belcher' or 'Screaming Skull' I was on my way!







This is Mina's very own mini, she's 8!
